Top 7 All-in-One Desktop Computers to Buy in Ghana Today

September 08, 2025

Introduction

All-in-one desktop computers are becoming the go-to computing solution for many Ghanaian students, professionals, and business owners alike, who seek sleek, space-saving technology. By combining the monitor and CPU into one compact unit, AIO PCs reduce clutter while offering powerful performance and modern features for everyday use. Whether you’re a creative professional, running an office, or setting up a home workstation, all-in-one desktops deliver efficiency without the bulk of traditional setups. If you’re shopping in Ghana for a reliable and stylish all-in-one PC, this guide will help you choose the right one for your needs.

What to Look for in an All-in-One PC (Performance, Display, Price)

When shopping for an AIO desktop, here are the main factors to consider:

  • Performance: Processor (Intel, AMD, or Apple M1), RAM, and storage.
  • Display: Size, resolution, touch capabilities, and color accuracy.
  • Price: Choose a machine that balances your budget with your needs.
  • Design & Portability: Ultra-thin and modern designs are great for small spaces.
  • Ports & Connectivity: USB-C, HDMI, Ethernet, and Wi-Fi are essential.

Below are the top 7 all-in-one desktops available for purchase in Ghana today.

1. HP All-in-One 24-df1036ny – Sleek & Powerful

This HP AIO delivers a perfect balance of style and performance. Featuring an Intel Core i5 processor, 8GB of RAM, and a 1TB hard drive, it is ideal for home users and office tasks. The 24-inch Full HD display provides crisp visuals for documents, browsing, and entertainment.

Key Features:

  • 24” Full HD display
  • Intel Core i5 processor
  • 8GB RAM, 1TB storage
  • Built-in webcam and speakers
  • Affordable for its specs

Perfect for everyday productivity, online meetings, and multimedia consumption.

2. Dell Inspiron 24 5000 AIO – Stylish and Office-Ready

Known for its premium design and dependable hardware, the Dell Inspiron 24 5000 is ideal for office professionals. It offers solid performance with options for Intel Core i5 or i7 processors and SSD storage for faster boot times.

Key Features:

  • 24” anti-glare Full HD display
  • Optional touchscreen
  • Up to an Intel Core i7 processor
  • SSD + HDD hybrid storage options
  • Sleek, minimalistic stand

This is a good choice if you need an elegant system that complements your office setup.

3. Apple iMac 24” (M1) – Premium Choice for Creatives

If you’re a creative professional in Ghana working with graphics, video, or music, the Apple iMac 24” is unmatched. Powered by Apple’s M1 chip, it delivers lightning-fast performance, a stunning 4.5K Retina display, and vibrant color accuracy.

Key Features:

  • 24” 4.5K Retina display
  • Apple M1 chip for incredible speed
  • 8GB/16GB unified memory
  • Ultra-slim design in vibrant colors
  • Magic Keyboard and Magic Mouse included

Perfect for graphic designers, video editors, and anyone who needs high performance with aesthetic appeal.

4. Lenovo IdeaCentre AIO 3 – Best Value for Students

For students looking for an affordable yet capable AIO, the Lenovo IdeaCentre AIO 3 is a solid pick. It features a compact design, AMD Ryzen processors, and an adjustable display, making it ideal for small study spaces.

Key Features:

  • 21.5” or 24” Full HD display
  • AMD Ryzen 3/5 processors
  • 4GB-8GB RAM with HDD or SSD
  • Compact and lightweight design
  • Excellent budget-friendly choice

It’s great for basic school tasks, online classes, and light entertainment.

5. ASUS AIO V241EA – Ultra-Thin with Full HD Display

ASUS is known for blending design and technology, and the V241EA doesn’t disappoint. With an ultra-slim bezel and immersive sound system, this AIO is ideal for home offices and casual use.

Key Features:

  • 23.8” Full HD NanoEdge display
  • Intel Core i3/i5 processors
  • Dual storage options (SSD + HDD)
  • ASUS SonicMaster stereo audio
  • Thin, modern design

Perfect for those who value aesthetics as much as functionality.

6. Acer Aspire C24 – Budget-Friendly and Efficient

The Acer Aspire C24 is an affordable yet efficient choice for users who need a reliable system for light tasks. It offers a sleek profile and solid performance at a reasonable price point.

Key Features:

  • 23.8” Full HD display
  • Intel Pentium Gold/Core i3
  • 4GB-8GB RAM, SSD storage
  • Compact, clutter-free setup
  • Ideal for small offices, home users, and budget-conscious buyers

7. MSI Modern AM242 – Ideal for Business Use

For businesses that need durable and efficient systems, the MSI Modern AM242 delivers. It’s designed for productivity with an anti-flicker display and energy-efficient operation.

Key Features:

  • 23.8” IPS-grade Full HD display
  • Intel 11th Gen processors
  • Silent cooling technology
  • Anti-glare and anti-flicker screen
  • Durable for long working hours

Best suited for professional environments where reliability and performance are paramount.

Where to Buy These AIO Desktops in Ghana (Online & In-Store)

You can find these all-in-one desktops at trusted electronics and IT retailers across Ghana, such as:

  • IPMC Showrooms: East Legon, Accra & other locations
  • Electroland Ghana: Samsung and HP AIOs
  • CompuGhana: Various brands
  • Online Stores: Jumia Ghana, IPMC online store, and other verified e-commerce platforms.

When buying online, always check for warranty, delivery options, and authenticity.

Current Price Range of AIO Desktops in Ghana

Prices of AIO PCs in Ghana vary based on brand, configuration, and store. As of 2025, here’s a rough guide:

  • Budget Range (Basic AIOs): GHS 4,000 – GHS 6,500
  • Mid-Range (Office-ready, better specs): GHS 7,000 – GHS 10,000
  • Premium (Apple iMac, high-end Dell & HP): GHS 15,000 – GHS 20,000+

Conclusion: Which All-in-One Desktop Is Best for You?

Your choice ultimately depends on your needs and budget:

  • Students & light users: Lenovo IdeaCentre AIO 3 or Acer Aspire C24
  • Office professionals: Dell Inspiron 24 5000 or HP AIO 24-df1036ny
  • Creatives: Apple iMac 24” (M1)
  • Businesses: MSI Modern AM242 or NAS-compatible setups

All-in-one desktops offer excellent functionality, a modern look, and a space-saving design, making them perfect for Ghanaian households and workplaces alike. For authentic products, warranties, and expert advice, consider visiting IPMC Showroom, CompuGhana, or other trusted stores.

Upgrade your workspace today with an all-in-one desktop that meets your performance and style needs.
