Let's start with what's revolutionizing internet connectivity in Ghana: Starlink satellite internet systems.

What you get

  • Starlink base unit with router
  • Mounting equipment for rooftops or walls
  • Wi-Fi boosters to improve indoor signal range

Whether you're in East Legon or Ejisu, Starlink connectivity solutions from IPMC Kart make it easier to stay online with uninterrupted speeds. This device is ideal for remote workers, content creators, and rural businesses.

Why it's trending: Affordable high-speed internet across Ghana.

2 Smartphones & Tablets (Apple, Samsung, Tecno)

When it comes to top electronic devices Ghanaian consumers rely on daily, smartphones and tablets are high on the list. At IPMC Kart, you'll find:

  • Apple iPhones (iPhone 13, 14, and SE models)
  • Samsung Galaxy A, M, and S series
  • Tecno and Infinix smartphones for budget users
  • Tablets for both productivity and leisure: iPad, Samsung Galaxy Tab, Lenovo Tab

These devices come in various specs and price ranges, and IPMC Kart also offers accessories such as screen protectors, cases, chargers, and styluses.

Why it's trending: Everyone wants to stay connected, work on the go, or watch content seamlessly.

3 Laptops (HP, Dell, Lenovo)

Whether you're a student, entrepreneur, software developer, or office executive, you need a reliable laptop. IPMC Kart stocks a wide variety, including:

  • HP Pavilion and ProBook
  • Dell Inspiron, Latitude, and XPS
  • Lenovo ThinkPad and IdeaPad

From ultra-slim machines for mobility to powerful laptops for gaming or programming, IPMC has something for everyone.

Plus, you get tech support, warranty service, and bundled accessories such as laptop bags, USB hubs, and wireless mice.

Why it's trending: Remote work, school, and content creation demand powerful portable machines.

4 Refrigerators (Nasco, LG)

Appliances have become more intelligent and energy-efficient, and refrigerators are no exception.

IPMC Kart offers compact and large-sized fridges from:

  • Nasco: Affordable and reliable for families and students
  • LG: Premium fridges with smart temperature control, inverter compressors, and sleek designs

You can also choose between:

  • Top-freezer and bottom-freezer styles
  • Single- or double-door models
  • Energy-saving features and child locks

Why it's trending: Electricity costs in Ghana are rising, and newer fridges save more power.

5 Air Conditioners

With Ghana's tropical climate, a good air conditioning system is a must-have, especially during the Harmattan, or dry seasons.

At IPMC Kart, you can purchase:

  • Split ACs and window units
  • Energy-efficient inverter ACs
  • Smart ACs with remote/wi-fi control

Brands like Nasco, Samsung, and LG are popular choices. The showroom also offers installation support and tips for optimal placement and maintenance.

Why it's trending: Heatwaves and long summers have made ACs a necessity in homes and offices.

6 Home Audio Systems

Upgrade your entertainment setup with premium sound systems. IPMC Kart has a lineup of:

  • JBL, LG, and Nasco soundbars
  • Home theaters with surround sound
  • Bluetooth stereo speakers and subwoofers

Whether you're setting up your living room, outdoor party, or church auditorium, there's an audio solution for every scenario.

Many devices also support USB, Bluetooth, HDMI, and wireless casting, allowing you to play music from any device.

Why it's trending: Music is central to Ghanaian culture, and people want immersive sound experiences.

7 Microwaves & Cookers

For busy professionals, students, and families, kitchen appliances that save time are lifesavers. IPMC Kart offers:

  • Microwaves with grill function
  • Electric and gas cookers
  • Induction stoves for safer and efficient cooking

With models from LG, Nasco, Samsung, and more, users enjoy features like child locks, fast-cook buttons, and even smart sensors.

Whether you're heating fufu or reheating soup, these gadgets are convenient, safe, and energy-saving.

Why it's trending: Urban dwellers prefer speed, safety, and reliability in cooking.

8 Gaming Consoles (PS5, Xbox)

Ghana's gaming community is growing, and IPMC Kart caters to both casual and pro gamers. Currently in stock:

If you're searching for trending electronics that Accra gamers are buying, these consoles are top sellers.

Why it's trending: Esports, YouTube gaming, and next-gen graphics have made gaming a lifestyle.

9 Printers & Office Electronics

Every modern home or office in Ghana needs a printer, scanner, or copier, especially with remote work, homeschooling, and small businesses booming.

IPMC Kart offers:

  • HP and Canon printers (Inkjet, Laser, and All-in-One)

You also get toner refills, ink cartridges, and technical support.

Why it's trending: Remote work and education require a mini office setup at home.

10 Smartwatches and Wearables

Stay fit, organized, and on time with smartwatches available at IPMC Kart:

  • Apple Watch Series 8 & SE
  • Samsung Galaxy Watch 5
  • Xiaomi Mi Band and Amazfit
  • Fitbit fitness trackers

These gadgets track your health, receive calls, and sync with your smartphone. With features like GPS, waterproofing, step counters, and heart-rate monitoring, wearables are more useful than ever.

Why it's trending: Ghanaians are becoming more health-conscious and tech-savvy.

Tips for Buying Electronics Devices in Ghana

Here are a few expert tips to help you make smart purchases:

  • Always compare prices across multiple stores.
  • Read reviews or ask for customer testimonials before purchase.
  • Ask for a product demonstration before leaving the showroom.
  • Check warranty policies carefully (ask what's covered and for how long).
  • Sign up for store promotions: some offer 5–10% off for first-time buyers or bulk purchases.
